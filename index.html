7 Days of Alignment & Accountability

A guided ascent through the Four Points. I spot you on every rep until you are ready to take the lead.

Day 1: The Anchor

Point 1: Where are we going?

The Sherpa's Cue: In every meeting today, anchor tactical tasks to the North Star. Ask: "How does this specific task get us to our destination?"
The Debrief: Did the anchor hold? If the team couldn't link their work to the "Where," we increase the weight tomorrow.
Day 2: The Window

Point 2: How long do we have?

The Sherpa's Cue: Ask your team: "What is the hard deadline for our #1 priority?" If answers differ, you're climbing at different speeds.
The Debrief: Consistency is safety. Did you find drift in the timeline?
Day 3: The Gear

Point 3: What do we need?

The Sherpa's Cue: Perform a gear check. Ask: "Do you have the tools, time, or support to finish this?" Don't accept 'I think so.'
The Debrief: Clarity on resources is the difference between a summit and a rescue.
Day 4: The Crevasse

Point 4: What is stopping us?

The Sherpa's Cue: Identify hidden obstacles. Ask: "If we fail this week, what will be the reason?" Call out the friction points now.
The Debrief: Addressing the crevasse before you reach it is how you build trust.
Day 5: The Safety Line

The Ownership Loop

The Sherpa's Cue: One person per deliverable. Shared responsibility is a frayed rope. Clarify ownership now.
The Debrief: Did your team feel held or helped?
Day 6: Handing Over

Teach to Lead

The Sherpa's Cue: Today, you step back. Watch a junior leader drill the Four Points. Observe their form.
The Debrief: You are moving from climber to guide.
Day 7: The Summit

Confirm the Route

The Sherpa's Cue: Review the week. Prepare your alignment script for next week. You are leading.
The Final Debrief: The muscle is built. Ready for the next ascent?
